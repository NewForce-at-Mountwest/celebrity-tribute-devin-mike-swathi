About Danny


Danny DeVito Jr. was born on November 17, 1944 in Neptune Township, NJ. He grew up in a family of five, with 2 older sisters. He shares a proud Italian heritage with his family, who are originally from San Fele, Basilicata and Calabria.

Danny was raised Catholic and attended boarding school when he was 14. After graduating, he trained at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts, which cultivated his passion and talent for acting.

Danny DeVito has had a very successful career both in front of the camera, and behind it whilst running the show. He currently lives in California to be closer to many acting opportunities in Hollywood.

​

When he was younger, Danny DeVito begged his father to be sent to boarding school to "keep him out of trouble". Clearly, it worked and he has many renowned works to show for it.


Family

  • Rhea Perlman (m.1982)
    American actress and author, Rhea Perlman met Danny DeVito when she went to see the play The Shrinking Bride, which he was in. Shortly afterwards, they moved in together and got married in 1982. They announced their separation a few years ago, but firmly stand on not getting legally divorced. They share a lot of love and history and share amiable terms as close friends.
